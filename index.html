Our apps do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Our apps are designed to function entirely on your device. We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)

2. Data Storage

Any data generated by our apps (such as scores, settings, or reminders) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics
  • Facebook SDK or Meta Pixel
  • Any advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data

Our apps are distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain information as described in Apple's Privacy Policy. This is beyond our control and subject to Apple's own privacy practices.

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal information from any users, our apps are safe for users of all ages.
