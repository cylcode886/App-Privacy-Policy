Privacy Policy

YILI CHO (cylcode) — iOS Apps

Last Updated: April 30, 2026
Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all iOS applications developed and published by YILI CHO (cylcode) on the App Store, including but not limited to apps in the productivity, music, fitness, gaming, and lifestyle categories.

Thank you for using our apps (each, the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what data we collect and how it is used. This policy covers all apps developed by YILI CHO (cylcode).

1.Information We May Collect

Depending on the App and the features you use, we may collect the following types of information. Not every app collects all of these — many of our apps collect only the minimum needed to display ads.

  • Account Information — If an app offers account creation, we may collect your name, email address, and optionally your age or gender (used for fitness-related calculations).
  • Activity and Health Data — For fitness or wellness apps, we may store data you choose to log: workout duration, steps, distance, medication schedules, or similar inputs. This data stays on your device unless you actively choose to sync it.
  • Device Information — Device model, operating system version, language settings, and identifiers such as IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) or IDFV (Identifier for Vendors).
  • Usage Data — Anonymous information about how you interact with the App, including features used, session length, and crash reports, used to improve stability and design.
  • Approximate Location — Derived from your IP address by ad and analytics providers, used to determine which ads can be shown in your region.

2.How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core features.
  • To calculate and display fitness or activity metrics (where applicable).
  • To send important system notifications and security alerts.
  • To analyze usage trends and improve product design.
  • To deliver and measure advertisements via Google AdMob.

3.Advertising and ATT

All of our apps display advertisements through Google AdMob. In line with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework:

  • If you allow tracking, AdMob may use your IDFA to show personalized ads relevant to your interests.
  • If you decline tracking, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized.
  • You can change this preference anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

4.Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with the following trusted service providers strictly for the purposes described:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics (where used) — for understanding user engagement and stability. Privacy Policy
  • Apple iCloud (where used) — for syncing your data across your own devices. We do not have access to data stored in your iCloud.

These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and may not use your data for purposes other than the services they provide to us.

5.Data Security and Retention

We use industry-standard security measures to protect any information collected. We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You can delete your in-app data at any time through the App's settings, or by contacting us directly for account-based data.

6.Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Update — Review or change your information within the App settings, where applicable.
  • Data Deletion — Delete your in-app data anytime, or contact us to request deletion of any account-based data.
  • Tracking Preferences — Manage tracking via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

7.Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, the information will be deleted promptly.

8.Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL. Material changes will be reflected in an updated effective date above.

9.Contact

Questions or concerns? Email us at cylcode@icloud.com
